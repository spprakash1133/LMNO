Why this works

"It doesn't optimise you. It simply appears — and asks for one thing."

LMNO philosophy

Lemeno doesn't track you

No analytics. No behavioural data. No profile. LMNO does not know what tabs you have open, what you clicked, or how long you spent anywhere.

It doesn't optimise you

There are no nudges, scores, streaks, or performance dashboards. LMNO is not trying to make you better. It's just asking you to breathe.

It simply appears

When the interval you set is up, LMNO shows up. No notifications. No guilt. No gamification. Just a cat, and a prompt.

Then it disappears

Once you've taken your breath, it's gone. You continue exactly where you were. No interruption beyond the pause itself.

What is LMNO?

A small reset.
That's it.

LMNO is a Chrome extension designed to help you take real breaks during screen time.

Instead of passive reminders, LMNO gently pauses your screen at intervals you choose — from 30 seconds to 10 minutes — and guides you through a simple breathing moment:

→ exhale

It's built for people who spend long hours online and forget to pause. No tracking. No distractions. Just a small reset that helps you refocus and continue.
