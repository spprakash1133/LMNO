FAQ

Common questions.

Is LMNO free?

Yes, completely free. If it helps you, you can optionally buy me catfood for $2 — that's it.

Does it collect my data?

No. LMNO works entirely locally in your browser. Nothing is ever sent anywhere.

Can I customise timing?

Yes. You can choose how frequently LMNO pauses your screen.

Will it interrupt important tasks?

You can snooze or skip any break. LMNO is a gentle nudge, not a lock-screen.

Which browsers are supported?

Currently Chrome only. Firefox and Edge versions are on the roadmap.

How do I uninstall it?

Right-click the extension icon in Chrome and select "Remove from Chrome". Done.
