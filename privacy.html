Privacy Policy

Short version: we don't collect anything. Here's the full version anyway.

The simple version LMNO is a Chrome extension that runs entirely inside your browser. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data — ever. This policy explains that in more detail.

What we collect

Nothing. LMNO does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry of any kind.

There are no servers receiving data from your browser. No third-party analytics scripts. No crash reporting that phones home.

What the extension stores locally

LMNO uses Chrome's local storage API solely to remember your preferences (e.g. chosen break interval) on your own device. This data:

  • Never leaves your browser
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the extension

Permissions we request

LMNO requests only the minimum Chrome permissions required to function:

  • storage — to save your timing preferences locally
  • alarms — to trigger breaks at your chosen interval
  • tabs — to display the break overlay on the active tab

None of these permissions are used to read, access, or transmit your browsing data.

Third-party services

The LMNO extension itself uses no third-party services.

This website (lmno.life) uses no tracking cookies, advertising networks, or analytics platforms. The only external resources loaded are Google Fonts (for typography), which is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

Children's privacy

LMNO does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The extension is suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we ever update this policy, we'll update the date below and post the new version here. Since we collect no data, changes are unlikely to be significant.

Contact
